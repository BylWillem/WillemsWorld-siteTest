Stage Eight of Eight

Consumption

Eight stages later, the product reaches a buyer. But who is that buyer, and how do they actually purchase it? The answer differs sharply across our three streams. Some products pass through millions of households at the grocery store; others are bought in bulk by procurement officers you've never met. The same supply chain story can end in very different places.

Block 01

How it's consumed

Before getting into who buys these products and how they place an order, the most tangible question: how do they end up in someone's hands? Three completely different last-mile experiences — pallet freight to a loading dock, parcel delivery to a doorstep, or no delivery at all because the buyer walks into a store and picks it off the shelf.
